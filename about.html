COVID-19 monitor

This dashboard visualises the weekly rate of coronavirus cases identified by pillar 1 and 2 testing in English local authorities. All the data derives from Public Health England. Further information about coronavirus can be found at: gov.uk/coronavirus

Notes
  • Only confirmed cases reported 5 or more days ago are regarded by Public Health England as complete. Cases reported over the last 5 days are coloured grey in the bar chart.
  • The seven-day average is the average of a day and the previous six days of confirmed cases.
